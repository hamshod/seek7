Electrolysis

The path to a better future

Defenition of ELECTROLYSIS:

1. A way to split H2O into 2H+O by running a current through it.

H2O --> 2H+O

2. A method of creating combustion energy using only water and less energy than was used.

H2O --> Sustainable energy!
